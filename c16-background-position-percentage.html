Magnolia

Magnolia grandiflora, commonly Known as the Southern magnoliaor bull bay,is a tree of the family Magnoliaceae native to the southeastern United States. From coastal Virginia south to central Florida. and west to eastern Texas and Oklahoma. Reaching 27.5 m (90 ft) in height. it is a large striking evergeen tree with large dark green leaves and large white fragrant flowers. Widely cultivated around the world, over a hundred cultivars have been bred and marketed commercially. The timber is hard and heavy, and has been used commercially to make furniture , pallets, and venner.
